    About Us

    At Western Palestra Academy, we provide a Multi sports Training Network with coaches specialized in thier respective sports. Not only playing sports is important, but also learning it in the correct way. For that, we have our curriculum and coaches trained specifically to improve in the opted sport area and fitness. Not only this, we also make up our own drills for students to get speed, strength and ability to top all the different categories in all sports. They will all develop as versatile players of that sport.

    We already have trained many players for many different sports. Many primary and pre - primary children and their parents are satisfied with thier progress. To be more specific, we have our age group as 4 -18. All children under this category are welcome.

    Along with education, sports plays an important role in our lives. While education enriches our minds, sports vitalize our bodies and keeps us healthy. We can say sports as a form of competitive physical activity played by the people through casual or organized participation. It encourages teamwork and sportsman spirit among players.

    While playing, discipline among players should be accompanied with fun and learning activities other than the game like personality building through the game itself. This is what we offer at our academy.
